Catch The Letter

IT Term 4 Project

Welcome to Catch the Letter!

Go To Game (warning, in Beta)

Catch the Letter is a vocabulary building game designed and built by Comic Sans Studios and MSPaint Studios. Catch the Letter was designed to help children in years 4 to 6 build and consolidate their "word database" or vocabulary.

System Requirements

Minimum:

  • Windows XP Service Pack 3 or newer
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 1 GHz Processor
  • A HTML5 compatible browser (we recommend Mozilla Firefox 30+)
  • The latest version of your graphics card drivers

Recommended:

  • Windows 7 or newer
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 2 GHz dual-core processor
  • A HTML5 compatible browser (we recommend Mozilla Firefox 30+)
  • A nVidia or AMD graphics card with latest drivers. We recommend you avoid any hardware using Intel graphics chips due to their poor performance, stability, and feature set.
  • A hi-speed broadband internet connection
