Catch The Letter

IT Term 4 Project

Welcome to Catch the Letter!

Go To Game

Catch the Letter is a vocabulary building game designed and built by Comic Sans Studios and MSPaint Studios. Catch the Letter was designed to help children in years 4 to 6 build and consolidate their "word database" or vocabulary.

About the Staff

Brad Fuller (@BradFuller)

Brad is the founder and CEO of MSPaint Studios, Inc. His studios have produced different print materials (brochures, posters, flyers, business cards), and full websites for various clients. He won the ICAS award for Best Speller in Australia of the Year in 2007, which is one of the main reason he was picked to develop this game. He has 14 years of computer experience, and has been developing games since 2008 using tools such as GameMaker, Unity3D and Unreal Engine 3.
